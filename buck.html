The Night Sky Darker

By J.W. Buchwalter

The Reclamation War is over, and the noble houses that led it are dead or scattered. Ther Immortale Absolute, the god-king of the Fereation, has secured his rule over those blashemers and heratics that defied him.

Liam Flynn has been running from the aftermath of that war ever since, the guilt of what he's done always chasing close behind. He needs one last job, one last big payout, to finance the venture to rescue his wife from the Federation and finally make things right.

Tassiana Forsyth is the last of her name, and she was just a child when the Immortal Absolute burned her homeworld for its defiance. She knows of a secret buried in those ashes that can start the war anew, but first, she must find a Whisperer. That being of magic and metal can wield a weapon fit to kill a god,

Artemis Reiner never waivered in his devotion to the Federation, but he did fail. He yearns to redeem himself in the eyes of the Immortal Absolute, no matter how much blood he has to spill to do it.
